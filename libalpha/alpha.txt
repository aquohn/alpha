Tree model: empty diagram is root, siblings are and-ed together
Clicking on an element selects the subtree rooted there

m: toggle mode

Erasure/Insertion:
d: delete (any even level graph; deleted graph may be pasted)
i: insert (add arbitrary child within odd level)

Iteration/Deiteration:
x: cut (can be pasted; sometimes must be pasted)
y: yank/copy 
p: paste 

Double Cut:
C: enclose with double cut
c: remove double cut (if top two nodes are cuts)

Insert:
a: append (click anywhere and type identifier)
C: add cut around selection
d: delete (any graph)
